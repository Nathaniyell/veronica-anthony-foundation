About Us

The Veronica Anthony Foundation is dedicated to creating lasting positive change in communities through education, health initiatives, and sustainable development.

Our Mission

To promote sustainable development by empowering individuals and communities through innovative programs in education, climate action, clean energy, health, and livelihood enhancement. We are committed to fostering self-reliance, reducing inequalities, and creating resilient systems that protect the environment while improving quality of life.

Our Vision

To build empowered individuals, thriving communities, and a sustainable environment that collectively ensures a brighter future for generations to come.

Our Focus Areas

Discover how we're making a difference through our key initiatives and programs

Education

Healthcare

Nutrition

Promoting better dietary habits and implementing nutritional programs for communities.

Renewable Energy

Exploring innovative ways to harness renewable energy for practical applications and fostering sustainability.

Clean Cooking
