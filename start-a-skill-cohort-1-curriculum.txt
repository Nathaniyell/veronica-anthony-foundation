PROJECT OBJECTIVES
================================================================================
• Bridge the digital skills gap by training youth in practical, market-ready digital tools over the programme lifecycle (pathway toward 1,000 youth in 2 years).
• Improve workplace readiness and professional branding.
• Boost confidence so participants are prepared for the digital economy.

================================================================================
KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS (TARGETS)
================================================================================
• Minimum 300 youth enrolled
• At least 85% completion rate
• 80% demonstrate improved digital competency (pre/post assessment)
• 70% develop optimised CV and LinkedIn profile
• 50% secure internship, freelance, or employment opportunities within 6 months
• 90% participant satisfaction rate

================================================================================
YEAR 1 IMPACT (INDICATIVE)
================================================================================
• 300–500 youth trained virtually across Nigeria
• At least 240 participants demonstrate measurable digital improvement
• 150+ participants secure economic opportunities within 6 months
• Increased income-earning capacity among training participants
